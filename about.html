Our Story

From humble beginnings to transformative impact - the journey of Khana Bank Trust

2018 - The Beginning

Founded in Varanasi with a simple mission: no one should go to bed hungry. Started with serving 50 meals daily to the underprivileged.

2019 - Expansion

Expanded operations to 5 communities, launched mobile food units, and began nutrition education programs.

2020 - Crisis Response

During the pandemic, adapted quickly to provide emergency relief, distributed over 100,000 meals during lockdown.

2021 - Innovation

Launched community gardens project, introduced sustainable farming techniques, and started vocational training programs.

2024 - Present

Now serving 50+ communities with 500+ volunteers, having touched over 100,000 lives with comprehensive food security programs.
