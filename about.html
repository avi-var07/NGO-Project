Our Mission & Vision

Driven by compassion and guided by hope, we work towards a world where no one sleeps hungry

Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about the detrimental effects of food waste on the environment, economy, and society. Through education, advocacy, and community engagement, we aim to promote responsible consumption, minimize food waste generation, and maximize food recovery for those in need.

Our Vision

To create a world where every individual has access to nutritious food, where communities are empowered to be self-sufficient, and where hunger becomes a memory of the past through sustainable development and social innovation.

Our Story

From humble beginnings to transformative impact - the journey of Khana Bank Trust

7th August 2018 - The Beginning

Founded in PDDU Nagar (Mughalsarai), with a simple mission: no one should go to bed hungry. Started with serving 50 meals daily to the underprivileged.

3rd December 2018 - First Mega Blood Donation Camp

Conducted the first mega blood donation camp.

January, 2019

Life of Jeevan of a cute Child -

24 July 2019 - Awareness Seminar on Food Waste and Management

Conducted Food Wastage, Need and Maintainence seminar to spread awareness among the youths.

11 February 2020 - Awareness for Prevention of COVID-19

Spread awareness of the ongoing pandemic crisis and release of emergency funds and services.

14th June 2019 - Corona Warriors

Provide free meals, medicines, masks, sanitizers and other medical equipments to the people. Appreciated for fighting the pandemic crisis.

24th December 2020 - Shelter House For the Needy Ones

Built KHANA BANK RAIN BASERA to act as a shelter to provide relief to the people fighting with extreme cold and weather.

2018 - Present

Now serving 50+ communities with 50+ volunteers, having touched over 100,000 lives with comprehensive food security programs.
