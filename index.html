About Khana Bank Trust

Khana Bank Trust is a dedicated non-profit organization founded with the vision of creating a hunger-free society. We believe that access to nutritious food is a fundamental human right, and we work tirelessly to ensure no one goes to bed hungry. We are committed to addressing the pressing issue of food waste and management. With a vision of creating a world where no food goes to waste and every person has access to nutritious meals, we strive to implement sustainable solutions at the grassroots level.

Our comprehensive approach includes food distribution, community gardens, nutrition education, and sustainable development programs that empower communities to become self-sufficient.
